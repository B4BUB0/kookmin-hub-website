Frequently Asked Questions

Is the community only for Kookmin students?

Our community is primarily for Kookmin University international students, but we're always open to connecting with students from other universities!

Are the Discord and Telegram communities free to join?

Yes! All our communities are completely free. Just join the Discord server or Telegram group using the links on the community page.

Can I organize my own event?

We encourage members to organize events. Contact us through Discord or email and we'll help you promote your event.

Is this an official Kookmin University program?

We're a student-led community supported by the Kookmin University International Office. We work closely with the university to serve international students.

How can I contribute content or guides?

We'd love your contributions! Share your knowledge and experiences. Contact us with your ideas or share directly in our communities.

I have a problem or safety concern. Who do I contact?

Contact our moderators on Discord/Telegram or email us at info@kookmin-intl.com. We take all concerns seriously and respond promptly.
