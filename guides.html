Opening a Bank Account

  • Visit a major bank (KB, Shinhan, Woori) near campus
  • Bring your passport and student ID
  • Banks have English-speaking staff - ask for help
  • Process takes about 20-30 minutes
  • You'll get a debit card within 1-2 weeks

Money Transfer

  • Use Wise or OFX for international transfers (cheaper than banks)
  • Bank transfers: SWIFT code available at your bank
  • TransferWise has competitive rates for students
  • Plan ahead - international transfers take 1-3 business days

Common Questions

What if I get sick?

Go to nearby clinic or hospital. Your insurance covers most costs. Download Naver Map to find clinics.

How do I make Korean friends?

Join clubs, go to events, take classes, and use language exchange groups. Many Korean students want to learn English!

Can I work part-time?

Yes, with a work permit. International office can help with application. Common jobs: tutoring, restaurants, shops.

Where do I go shopping?

Myeongdong, Gangnam, Hongdae for clothes. HomePlus, Emart for groceries. Costco near university is great!

How to extend my visa?

Apply 1-2 months before expiration at Immigration Office. Bring university letter. Usually takes 1 week.

What about mail/packages?

Use convenience stores as collection points. Download convenience store apps to track shipments easily.
